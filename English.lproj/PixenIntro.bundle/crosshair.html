Alignment Crosshair and Right Tool

Pixen's Alignment Crosshair helps you keep your images straight and place your pixels just where you want them. It even expands to fit the tool you're using. Simply navigate to View > Show Alignment Crosshair to turn it on.

Right Tool

Pixen allows you to choose independent tools and colors for the left and right mouse button. One example: pencil on the left; eraser on the right. Just click the disclosure triangle in the Tools palette to get started.
