Color Palettes

Pixen's color palettes are key to the creation of low- and high-color pixel art alike. They make it easy to create and manage predefined schemes of colors that can be reused across documents.

To get started, open the Colors panel by clicking on the color well in the Tools palette. Switch to the Pixen Colors tab (on the far right).

Here, you can create and organize palettes of common colors, import palettes of various formats, and even detach palettes into their own panels. To make a color active, simply click it. To change a color, drag a replacement color on top of it.

Document windows also contain a color frequency palette. When you modify its colors, the image changes accordingly.
