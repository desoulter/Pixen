Tool Properties

Most tools have properties you can change to customize their behavior. You can open the Tool Properties panel by pressing Shift+⌘+T or double-clicking a tool.

Line width, fill style, and color tolerance are a few of the tool properties you'll find.

Patterns

The pencil, line, and eraser tools allow you to place a custom pattern (instead of a single pixel) while drawing. Just click the Set Pattern button in one of these tool's properties panels.

If you make a pattern you like, you can keep it around for later use or share it with others. To export a pattern, just drag it out of the pane on the right.
